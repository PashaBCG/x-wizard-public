# X-Wizard (Quick Start Guide)

**Anything you repeat every week — X-Wizard learns to do it for you.**

---

## What it does (when connected to your data)

- *"Send Sarah an e-mail with the latest staffing changes"*
- *"Save this instruction as a BCG-branded PDF"*
- *"What are the top-10 clients we are negotiating with right now"*
- *"Build me a PowerPoint from these bullet points"*
- *...*
